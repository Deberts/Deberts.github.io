How to Play

Deberts is a trick-taking card game played with a 32-card deck (7s to Aces). Here's how the game works:

  1. Game Setup: 2 to 4 players, each receives 6 cards.
  2. Trump Suit: The trump suit is determined by the cards dealt.
  3. Scoring: Earn points based on captured cards and combinations.
  4. Winning: The player/team with the highest score wins!
